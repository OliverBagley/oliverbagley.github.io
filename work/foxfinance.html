Fox Smart Finance

Fox is a collection of web based finance tools designed to help promote responsible budgeting and improve the spending habits of users. The application is designed to appeal to younger users and contains tools and information on how to make better financial decisions, plan for the future and live within their means. At launch Fox will contain a budget planner, loan calculator and tax calculation tool, with a blog and ever growing wiki page of financial advice. Fox is designed around primary user feedback and research in to what users want from a service, as well as an analysis of existing financial services and where these can be improved upon.
