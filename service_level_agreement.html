Revised Service Level Agreement (SLA)

Agreement Overview

This Service Level Agreement (SLA) is designed to clearly delineate the expectations and responsibilities of both the client and Ankura Services, to ensure the mutual interests are safeguarded.

Service Expectations

Clients are advised to anticipate minor delays in email responses as we prioritize dedicated, uninterrupted work periods to maintain our service quality.

Communication & Scheduling

To address your needs and respect our existing commitments, a minimum of 48 hours’ notice is requested, ideally 1-2 weeks, for graphic creation needs. For website development projects, the lead time extends to a minimum of 2 months, preferably 3-4 months, prior to the intended delivery date. We maintain our primary communication through email and shared documents on Google Drive, avoiding informal channels like Facebook and texting.

Billing & Reconciliation

We conduct bi-weekly billing and reconciliation, with provisions for modifications as needed. Payments are required immediately upon invoice receipt, with a 30-day grace period without interest. Automated reminders will be employed to ensure timely payments.

Detailed Clauses

Definitions
0.1 An SLA serves as a commitment, outlining coverage for various project-related concerns.
0.2 A “project” encompasses brands, websites, graphics, designs, including print materials, web graphics, content, and strategy.
0.3 “Impact” refers to actions from either party that hinder contractual fulfilment.
0.4 The term “contract” refers to the agreement between Ankura Services and the client, governed by the terms in this SLA.

Communication Protocols
1.1 Email responses are within 48 hours, with calls returned once daily.
1.2 Ankura Services operates on a weekly project scheduling basis, requiring clients to plan and agree on delivery dates.
1.3 Clients must provide essential details in requests, including deadlines, budget, objectives, list of required parts, technical specifications, and references.
1.4 Clients are responsible for providing all necessary materials before commencing work to avoid delays and redundancies.
1.5 Only formal requests via email or phone will be considered valid.

Contractual Obligations
2.1 These terms apply to all clients unless otherwise agreed in writing.
2.2 Acceptance of a quote implies agreement to this SLA's terms.
2.3 Ankura Services reserves the right to modify these terms after notifying clients.

Quotation & Costing
3.1 Initial estimates are provided based on conceptual discussions. Final amounts may vary based on actual work.
3.2 Any additional work will be confirmed and billed separately.
3.3 All shared documents and discussions are commercially confidential.
3.4 Requests added after project acceptance may incur additional charges.
3.5 Quotes are valid for 14 days unless otherwise stated.
3.6 Immediate payment of deposits is required, with the balance due upon completion.

Work Commencement
4.1 Written approval via email is needed before starting any work.

Subcontracting
5.1 Ankura Services may subcontract aspects of the project as needed.
5.2 Costs related to subcontracting will be recharged to the client after agreement.
5.3 Ankura Services is not responsible for the services provided by third-party contractors.

Variations
6.1 Changes to the fixed-price quotation will be evaluated independently.
6.2 Additional work will be evaluated and added to the final amount.

Design Services
7.1 Estimates for graphic design services are provided to the client.
7.2 Changes required after payment will incur additional charges.
7.3 Estimates will detail the client's position, design direction, and costs.
7.4 Extensive modifications outside the approved agreement will incur additional costs.

Websites
8.1 Details are as per the previous section, emphasizing the importance of clarity in specifications and estimates, addressing changes and upgrades, third-party services, support availability, and response times.

Copyright
9.1 Ankura Services holds intellectual property rights until the final payment.
9.2 Copyright transfers to the customer upon final payment.
9.3 Client-provided materials are the client’s property.
9.4 Ankura Services will not reuse client-provided content or images without permission.
9.5 Ankura Services may use projects for promotional purposes.

Hourly Rate Charges
10.1 Unquoted services will be billed at the standard hourly rate.
10.2 Hourly rates are reviewed annually.
10.3 Minimum billing is for 15 minutes, in 1-minute increments.

Approval
11.1 Details are as per the previous section, emphasizing the importance of careful review before approval and highlighting the implications of post-approval modifications.

Training
12.1 Training is provided for updating website sections via the content management platform.
12.2 Training is private and may be included in the initial agreement.
12.3 One hour of support is included post-training.

Hosting
13.1 Hosting includes unlimited disk space and bandwidth via third parties: GoDaddy and Flywheel.
13.2 Daily backups are performed for databases.
13.3 Cooperation is assured if a customer wishes to move their website.
13.4 Assistance for non-technical faults will be charged.
13.5 Hosting is billed monthly or annually.

Email Services
14.1 Ankura Services can set up email accounts, included in the website price.
14.2 Ankura Services is not liable for any interruption or termination of the email service.
14.3 Email problems are addressed based on the liability.

Search Engines
15.1 Basic SEO services are included.
15.2 Ankura Services is not responsible for actual rankings achieved.

Billing
16.1 Payment is in two parts: 50% initially and the final balance within 30 days of completion.
16.2 Invoices have a 30-day grace period.
16.3 Large projects may have multiple interim invoices.
16.4 Various payment methods are accepted.
16.5 Special credit terms must adhere to specified dates.
16.6 Non-payment consequences include service interruption, formal notice, and collection service involvement.
16.7 All design work is Ankura Services’ intellectual property until full payment is received.

Performance & Rectification
17.1 Details are as per the previous section, emphasizing the commitment to ensuring specification compliance and rectifying any reported issues.

Liabilities
18.1 Ankura Services is not liable for project delays or performance issues of developed entities/services.

Privacy
19.1 Customer information will only be used to provide services and products to the customer.

Contract Termination
20.1 Details are as per the previous section, outlining the conditions under which a contract can be terminated and the corresponding implications.
